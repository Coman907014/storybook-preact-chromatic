    <div className="row start-align col-lg-12 col-md-12 col-sm-12 py-8">Virgin Atlantic’s heading styles differ from body text through a combination of increased size and differing weight. This ensures they are distinct from body text but related to each other with typeface consistency, which helps users to scan content easily.</div>
